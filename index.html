Why did we decide to make this website?

With the rise of technology advancing at a rapid rate, and the threats towards data privacy increasing, it is becoming more apparent why security is becoming a larger concern. Passwords are one of the main defenses protecting our data against attackers.

Through a website, we hope to educate people regarding passwords, password salting, password hashing, and more, in an interesting and interactive way. Educating people on the importance of passwords is a solid start on being secure online.

This can serve as a way for a person to impact their social network in a positive way. A domino effect, where one person emphasizes collective responsiblity in cybersecurity, hopefully fostering a community of digital responsibility.
